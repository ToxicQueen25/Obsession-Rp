Server Rules

Luxury serious roleplay. Real stories. Real consequences. Respect the city, respect the scene, and protect the roleplay.

Introduction

Welcome to Obsession RP, where immersive storytelling and community respect form the core of our experience. These rules are a commitment to keeping the city fair, inclusive, and enjoyable for everyone.

Disclaimer

Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse. All members are expected to review this document and stay updated with changes. Staff reserves the right to use discretion when enforcing rules.

Important Notes

  • Rules are subject to change.
  • Roleplay always comes first.
  • Winning is never more important than the story.

Fear for Life & Combat Engagement

All players must roleplay a realistic fear of death or serious injury. Your character is not fearless, invincible, or immune to danger.

Prohibited Behavior

  • Do not rush multiple armed individuals alone.
  • Do not ignore weapons aimed at you.
  • Do not act fearless because of skill, armor, or experience.
  • Do not instantly re-engage after being injured, downed, or crashed.
  • Do not turn every confrontation into a gunfight.

Injuries & Recovery

After serious injury, crashes, explosions, or close calls, you must roleplay pain, disorientation, and recovery time.

New Life Rule

When your character is fully downed, declared dead, or respawned, they do not remember the events that led to their death.

  • No revenge or retaliation.
  • No returning to the death scene until at least 15 minutes have passed and the scene is over.
  • No sharing information from the incident.
  • No using radio, phone, or third parties to pass lost information.

Fail Roleplay

Fail RP happens when a player ignores realistic roleplay to gain an advantage, avoid consequences, or disrupt a scene.

  • Do not ignore injuries, restraints, fear, or threats.
  • Do not use glitches or mechanics to avoid RP.
  • Do not abandon scenes without valid RP reason.
  • Do not act on knowledge your character would not have.

Semi-Realistic Driving

Driving should stay reasonable for the situation and vehicle. Unrealistic stunts, extreme speeds through traffic, and reckless behavior are discouraged.

Speed Limits

  • City: 70 MPH
  • Sandy & Paleto: 70 MPH
  • Highways: 100 MPH

Aircraft

Aircraft may not land in the city except on approved helipads. Use Sandy, Grapeseed, or Los Santos International Airport.

Police Department Rules

Police are held to a higher roleplay and conduct standard because of their authority, equipment, and influence over scenes.

  • Police must prioritize realistic roleplay over winning scenes.
  • Officers must respond to active calls when on duty.
  • Use of force must be justified and progressive.
  • Firearms are a last resort.
  • Downed individuals are not active threats.
  • PD must allow meaningful roleplay opportunities.

Criminal Roleplay Rules

Criminal roleplay must be story-driven and purposeful, not chaos for content.

  • Crimes must have intent, planning, and risk.
  • Do not commit crimes only to provoke police.
  • Escalation must be gradual and justified.
  • Hostages must be treated as valuable lives.
  • Losing a scene is a valid roleplay outcome.

Dealing with Staff

  • Always follow staff direction.
  • Do not approach staff characters in city for OOC issues.
  • Open a ticket for reports, appeals, or concerns.
  • Evidence provided in reports is kept confidential.
  • Let scenes play out, gather evidence, then make a ticket.

Punishments

Punishments may include verbal discussions, community service, suspension, or permanent ban depending on severity.
