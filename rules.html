Welcome to Obsession RP

Obsession RP is a Military Family Owned & Operated community built around immersive storytelling, meaningful character development, and unforgettable roleplay experiences.

Every interaction, decision, and consequence helps shape the world we create together. Our goal is to provide a serious roleplay environment where stories matter and actions have meaning.

By joining Obsession RP, you agree to follow all server rules, Discord rules, and platform Terms of Service. Not knowing a rule does not excuse breaking it.

The Obsession Standard

Not every situation can be covered by a written rule. Players are expected to use common sense and make decisions that improve the roleplay experience for everyone involved.

Value of Life

Your character values their life just as a real person would. Fear, injury, danger, and risk should always be roleplayed realistically.

Characters are not superheroes. If you are outnumbered, outgunned, injured, or placed in a dangerous situation, your character should respond in a believable way.

Reckless actions without concern for personal safety, serious injury, or death may be considered Fail Roleplay.

New Life Rule

When your character experiences a complete loss of consciousness, death, or a full memory wipe situation, the events leading up to that incident are forgotten.

This includes names, faces, voices, vehicles, locations, conversations, and actions connected to the scene.

Lost information may not be shared through phone, radio, Discord, third parties, or any outside method. Memories may only return naturally through roleplay.

Roleplay Expectations

Obsession RP is built around immersive storytelling. Players are expected to participate in scenes honestly, realistically, and cooperatively.

Fail Roleplay happens when a player ignores realistic behavior, avoids consequences, abuses mechanics, or disrupts a scene to gain an advantage.

Injuries should be roleplayed. Threats should be taken seriously. Consequences should be accepted naturally. Winning should never matter more than the story.

Semi-Realistic Driving

Driving should remain realistic for the vehicle being used and the environment around it.

Major crashes, rollovers, and high-speed collisions should be roleplayed properly. Vehicles are not indestructible, and reckless driving without reason may be considered Fail Roleplay.

Aircraft should only land at approved airports, airfields, helipads, or realistic landing areas.

Law Enforcement Roleplay

Law Enforcement Officers are held to a higher roleplay standard because of their authority and responsibility within the city.

Officers should focus on professionalism, realism, and meaningful interactions instead of simply winning scenes or making arrests.

Use of force should be reasonable, justified, and appropriate for the situation. Escalation should happen naturally through roleplay.

Criminal Roleplay

Criminal roleplay should be purposeful, realistic, and story-driven. Crime should create roleplay, not chaos for the sake of chaos.

Robberies, kidnappings, gang activity, and other criminal scenes should involve planning, risk, and meaningful interaction.

Violence should not be the first answer to every problem. Losing a scene can create just as much story as winning one.

Staff & Community Support

The Obsession RP Staff Team exists to keep the community fair, welcoming, and enjoyable for everyone.

If an issue happens, allow the roleplay scene to finish, gather evidence, and submit a support ticket through Discord.

Players are expected to be respectful during staff interactions. Lying, manipulating evidence, or misleading staff may result in harsher punishment.

Final Thoughts

Obsession RP is more than just a server. It is a community built on creativity, respect, and storytelling.

Create stories. Respect others. Embrace consequences. Have fun building unforgettable moments together.
